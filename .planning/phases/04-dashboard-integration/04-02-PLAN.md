<objective>

reads the `report` query param, filters its list to that single report, and shows a dismissible
filter banner. Clearing the filter restores the full list.

Purpose: Completes the deep-link navigation initiated by the dashboard badge (04-01), making
         the cross-view flow functional end-to-end.
Output:  Modified MatcherView.vue with useRoute/useRouter, filterReportId computed,
         filtered matchedReports, filter-banner template, contextual empty state, and CSS.
</objective>
